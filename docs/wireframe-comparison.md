## **Design Highlights**

* **Fixed left column** shows metric names
* Scrollable horizontal layout for 2–4 scenarios
* Favorite stars and tags remain editable
* Each scenario has buttons to:

  * **Edit** (opens in form)
  * **Duplicate**
  * **Set as Baseline** for the group
* All revenue and margin metrics are clearly broken down by **month** and **year**
